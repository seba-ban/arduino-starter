13 fun Arduino projects for children aged ~8 and up.
No experience needed — every project has step-by-step wiring instructions and fully commented code.

**Board:** Arduino Uno R4 WiFi
**Language:** Simple English

## Suggested starting order

Start with ⭐ projects first — they are quick wins that feel rewarding!

**Absolute beginners:** 01 → 02 → 03 → 04
**Lights and colors:** 01 → 02 → 05 → 06
**Sounds and sensors:** 04 → 08 → 09
**Moving things:** 10 → 11
**Cool gadgets:** 07 → 12 → 13

After the ⭐ projects, pick any ⭐⭐ project that looks fun — they are all independent!

## What you need before starting

1. **Arduino IDE** installed on your computer (free at [arduino.cc](https://www.arduino.cc/en/software))
2. **Arduino Uno R4 WiFi** board and **USB A-C cable**
3. Components from the kit (each project lists exactly what it needs)

## Setting up Arduino IDE for the first time

1. Open Arduino IDE
2. Go to **Tools → Board → Boards Manager**
3. Search for **Arduino UNO R4**
4. Click **Install** next to **Arduino UNO R4 Boards**
5. Go to **Tools → Board → Arduino UNO R4 Boards** and select **Arduino UNO R4 WiFi**
6. Connect your Arduino with the USB cable
7. Go to **Tools → Port** and select the port that shows your Arduino

You are ready to go!
